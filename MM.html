Rubber Mixing Mill

  • John Doe
  • Jan 1, 2020
  • 12 Comments

Mixing mill as known for us is mainly used in rubber & plastic products factory, for instance Natural rubber, raw rubber and compound mixing, as well as rubber warm-up and rubber sheets. The rubber mixing mill machine is mainly used for rubber mat soak, mixed and natural rubber.

Rubber roller mill is made up of several important accessories. The hard tooth surface reducer, rollers, and the bearings all use the domestic well-known brands. Some important electrical parts like PLC are using international famous brands.

Machine of decelerate of hard tooth flank, roller, and bearing are using domestic famous brand, some important electrical parts, such as PLC is the use of international famous brand also is mainly composed of machine body with integrate in distance adjustable device, driving safety and emergency stop device, roll temperature adjusting device, lubricating system, rubber block and glue joint equipment, electrical control system.

Transmission system: machine of decelerate of hard tooth flank gear reducer, motor and reducer is connecting elastic ring coupling. Reducer adopts double output shaft structure and connection of gambler, nylon pin universal coupling. All of the drive gear reducer is gathered in housing, spray type, good lubrication and long life time.

Hard tooth type rubber mixing mill is used for the rubber compounding and sheeting
1. The rolls are made of chilled cast iron. The roll surface is hard and wearable. The internal cavity is processed to make temperature well proportioned on the roll surface.
2. The machine is equipped with an overload protection device to prevent the major components from being damaged due to overloading.
3. The machine is also equipped with and emergency device. When an emergent accident happens, just draw the pull rod, and the machine will stop immediately. It is safe and reliable.
4. The transmission system adopts a hard facing gear reducer, which has a compacted structure with higher transmission efficiency, lower noise and longer service life.
5. The base frame is a whole framework, which is convenient for installation
